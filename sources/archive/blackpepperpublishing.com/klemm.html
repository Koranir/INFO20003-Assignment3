Barry Klemm
Running Dogs
From soldier to radio dramatist to novelist

Barry Klemm was born in 1945. He abandoned an array of careers before concentrating on writing. He emerged in the 1990’s with a series of one hour radio dramas broadcast on Radio National. At the same time a number of short stories were published. He has published two teenage novels, The Tenth Hero and The Last Voyage of the Albatross. Running Dogs is his first adult novel. He is a Vietnam War veteran. Running Dogs is based on his experience in that conflict and was broadcast in serial form on Radio National Australia in 2006.
