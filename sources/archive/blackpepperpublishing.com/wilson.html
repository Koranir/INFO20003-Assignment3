Rhonda Wilson has always loved listening to people tell stories. She has worked as an actor, director, teacher and oral historian. With her theatre company ‘The Tide’ she has produced plays in Melbourne, Sydney and New York, including many of the works of the internationally acclaimed play-wright Daniel Keene. Her earlier oral history Good Talk, The Extraordinary Lives Of Ten Ordinary Australian Women (Penguin, 1995) was nominated for the Victorian Premier’s Prize in Australian Studies.
