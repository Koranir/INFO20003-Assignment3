Raphaëlle Pomian was born in 1956 in Nantes, France. She lived in Australia from 1976 until 1992. She co-wrote a play Landscapes of a Life with Graham Henderson which was performed at La Mama in 1988. She is a practising artist and has exhibited at the old Powell Street Gallery, Melbourne and in Paris. Don et Misères/Gifts & Sorrows was written during her sojourn here. She has continued to write in France.
