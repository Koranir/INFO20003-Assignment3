Jon Weaving was born in London in 1955 and now lives in Melbourne with his son. His short stories have appeared in both literary and popular magazines and have won numerous awards including the Olga Masters Bicentennial Award For Literature. His works include fiction, articles, anthologies, a creative writing text for secondary students and columns for a national magazine. Besides having judged many literary awards he has been awards numerous grants, worked as a writer in residence in many areas of Australia, and in 1994 was shortlisted for the Guiness Award. His thriller, To A Wolf The Kill, was published by William Heinemann Australia in 1997.
