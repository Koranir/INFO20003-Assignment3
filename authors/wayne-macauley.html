Wayne Macauley

About the Author

Wayne Macauley is a Melbourne writer whose stories have been published in Meanjin, Westerly, Overland, Arena, HQ and other magazines. He was the winner of The Age Short Story Competition in 1995 and was anthologised in Best Australian Stories 2001. He has written extensively for the theatre over many years and was a founding member of the award-winning site-specific performance company, the Institute of Complex Entertainment (winner of the 1999 Green Room Award for ' Tower of Light ’). His first novella was Blueprints for a Barbed-Wire Canoe (Black Pepper, 2004) and his second Caravan Story (Black Pepper, 2007). These two novellas have since been republished by Text Publishing. Other Stories (Black Pepper, 2010) collects his short fictions together and has received widespread critical acclaim.
