The Beech Resort

Margaret Mathews

Description

\"The car races. It swerves and passes. Warehouses, parks, shops. Brown, green, red. Brown, green, red. Blur, blur, blur. Then it slows and meanders through the last couple of back streets. And I’m there, here. Outside Bella’s.\"

When Bronwen falls in love with her therapist, Bella, Bronwen's husband is left hurt and confused. As the couple rages, the marriage disintegrates and Bronwen and Richard separate.

Bronwen suffers a nervous breakdown and depression and alcohol abuse spiral her into Beechwood psychiatric hospital. Her recovery is slow but is buoyed by the charismatic psychiatrist Boris and the 'alcohol doctor' Lenny. Together they help Bronwen remain sober and will her to a better future.

The sequel to the much-loved Room for Delusion, The Beech Resort continues to the explore the relationship between Bronwen and Bella as they trawl through the struggles and problems encountered within Bronwen's marriage and with insight and determination she becomes stronger.

"Told in a lively fashion and rich in sensual detail." The Age
