Mirranda Burton

About the Author

Mirranda Burton was born in Auckland, New Zealand in 1973 Since graduating from art school in South Australia in 1995, she has forged pathways in independent and commercial animation, multimedia design, printmaking and drawing. More recently she has devoted herself to the medium of comics, drawing upon her life experiences. Her comics have been published in Tango and Going Down Swinging since 2006, and in 2010 she won the Scenario Prize for her comic strip P.S. at the Fumetto International Comix Festival in Lucerne, Switzerland. Hidden is her first book. In September 2011 she was artist-in-residence at Dunmoochin where she had access to Clifton Pugh’s printing press. website: www.pawpedaller.com blog: Notes from a Pawpedaller. an example of recent work from Notes from a Pawpedaller
